Lasagna

Description

A lasagna is made by stacking layer of pasta sheet, meat sauce and bechamel sauce. It's not a recipe to prepare when you're in a hurry, but if you prepare more, you can freeze it to have it ready. The result is definitely worth the work!

Ingredients

  • For the meatsauce:
    • Meat
    • Onion and Garlic
    • Tomato sauce
  • For the bechamel sauce:
    • Milk
    • Butter
    • Flour
    • Nutmeg
  • Lasagna pasta

Steps

  1. Prepare the bechamel sauce:
    1. Put the milk in a pan to warm at luke warm temperature. Don't let it boil.
    2. Add butter in another pan and let it melt on medium low heat.
    3. When it's melted, add the flour and start mixing until a paste is created.
    4. Now add the milk and stir until it becomes a cream.
    5. Keep cooking to let it ticken and add nutmeg and pepper at the end. Set it aside
  2. Prepare the meat sauce:
    1. Put onion and garlic in a pan with oil and let them saute at low heat.
    2. Add the meat and stir. Let it brown.
    3. Add the tomato sauce and let it cook for 30 minutes.
  3. Assemble the lasagna
    1. Put some meat sauce and bechamel in a large bowl. Mix them together a bit
    2. Add the lasagna pasta to cover them.
    3. Add the two sauce and some cheese.
    4. Repeat until the bowl is full.
    5. Cook for 50 minutes at 200Â° C.
