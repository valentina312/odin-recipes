Lasagna

Description

A lasagna is made by stacking layer of pasta sheet, meat sauce and bechamel sauce. It's not a recipe for you're in a hurry, but if you prepare more, you can freeze it to have it ready. The result is definitely worth the work!

Ingredients

  • For the sauce:
    • Meat
    • Onion and Garlic
    • Tomato sauce
  • For the bechamel sauce
    • Milk
    • Butter
    • Flour
    • Nutmeg
  • Lasagna pasta
