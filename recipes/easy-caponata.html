Easy Caponata

Description

Caponata is a traditional Sicilian dish with vegetables cooked in oil and vinegar. Flavoured with many mediterranean ingredients, it's tangy, sweet and crunchy. You'll fall in love with it!

Ingredients

  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Eggplant
  • Red bell peppers
  • Zucchini
  • Tomato puree
  • Olives
  • White Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Raisins
  • Pine nuts
  • Capers

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil. Add celery and cook until softned. Drain.
  2. Heat oil in a large pain and add onion and celery and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add eggplant, red bell peppers and zucchini and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in tomato puree, vinegar and sugar. Cook for about 15 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Add olives, raisins, pine nuts and capers. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Cook for 5 minutes. Add chopped basil and serve at room temperature.
