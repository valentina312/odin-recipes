Chicken Tenders

Description

This recipe is so easy you won't believe it. In 10 minutes you'll have a yummy dish that with a side of salad will make the perfect dinner. The chicken tenders are coated in a crispy flavoured coat and cooked in the oven.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Chicken tenders
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Beat the egg in a bowl
  2. Put bread crumbs and all the spice in a zip bag
  3. Dip the chicken in the egg and then put it in the zip bag and shake to coat.
  4. Put the tenders on a foil lined baking sheet. Repeat for all the tenders.
  5. Sprinkle some oil on the tenders.
  6. Cook at 230Â° C for 7 minutes on each side.
